✦ How it all began

2017

It Started with a Bag of Beans

Nishan returned from Pokhara with single-origin Nepali coffee beans and a refusal to accept that Boudha didn't have a proper cafe. A tiny kitchen. A hand grinder. A dream.

the first bag
finding the space
2018

Finding the Space

A forgotten corner shop on Boudha-4. Wooden floors, cracked walls, and morning light that came in perfectly at 8am. Nishan signed the lease on a Thursday.

2019

The Door Opens

January 12th. Six tables, three coffees, and Chef Kamusari's first almond croissants. By noon the cafe was full. By evening they'd run out of everything. A perfect start.

opening day ♥
today
Today

A Community Ritual

Students, monks, travelers, locals — everyone calls this their table. Nishan Cafe is now Boudha's living room. The stupa bells, good coffee, better company.
